so much dead light / so intimate one feels like a voyeur / every door in the house wide open / thawed -- fought / purse -- burn -- bird / there’s nothing kind about resurrection / how the air refuses to shut behind her / evil given a name & feathers / it makes you sad, all the gouges in the wall / it gives me an excuse to fall back on

25 March 2016

These virgins
and their stagnant blood
hot and woolen
with shame

Had they learned how to swallow sooner
how to quiver like a fetal arrow
when first touched
to enjoy it

Instead of spending nights alone
amber foam filling each crease
of skin like soap

Would she still be

8 January 2016

the awful logic of parallel lines

-

enters with its tired mouth and says hello, offers a single thread laced around its clavicle so i play how i remember - wind my fingers through memory’s still-warm slots, watch our edges align in a curious act of reductive addition - and in pulling down, feel as new tissue opens spilling through every shy door in the distance
